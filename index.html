🛒 Materials You Need

Fun Foam Sheets Available from craft shops or Amazon

Ballpoint Pen To press designs into the foam
🎨
Ink Pads or Paint Different colours for layering
📄
Plain Paper For printing onto

What You Need To Do

1
Cut Your Foam

Prepare your foam sheets for stamp making:

  • Cut foam into manageable sizes (around 10x10cm works well)
  • You can make multiple smaller stamps or one larger one
  • Make sure the surface is clean and flat
2
Draw Your Design

Use a ballpoint pen to press your design into the foam:

  • Press firmly to create deep grooves
  • The pressed lines will stay white when you print
  • Think about patterns, textures, or shapes
  • Simple designs often work best!
3
Ink and Print

Apply ink and make your prints:

  • Press the foam onto an ink pad or apply paint with a brush
  • Press firmly onto paper
  • Make at least 3 different prints
  • Try different colours and layering
4
Scan or Photograph

Make digital copies of your best prints:

  • Scan at 300 DPI or higher
  • Or take clear, well-lit photos
  • Save as JPEG files
  • Make sure prints are flat and in focus
⚠️

Important Tips!

  • Press hard with the ballpoint pen — light lines won't show
  • Let ink dry completely before scanning
  • Don't use too much ink — it can blob and smudge
  • Keep your foam stamps — you'll use them again!
💡

Tips for Great Prints

  • Try overlapping prints for interesting effects
  • Use contrasting colours for layering
  • Print on different papers to see what works
  • Make more prints than you need — you can choose the best ones
  • Experiment with rotating your stamp
